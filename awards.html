Ricardo López-Crespo Award to STIMA2 Project

Premio Ricardo López-Crespo

The STIMA2 project (acronym in English for Single Tree Irrigation Management Algorithms for Almonds) aims to optimize irrigation water use efficiency in almond cultivation through the utilization of various technologies, such as drones equipped with thermal cameras, low-cost agroclimatic stations, and sensor networks for locally measuring farm meteorological conditions.

March 2023

MH to Master Final Project

Radiometric calibration of IR sensors on UAVs

In my Master's thesis work, I developed a calibration model for thermal infrared cameras mounted on UAVs. The significant difference between the temperatures generated from a thermal orthomosaic and the temperatures on the ground has made aerial thermography work very slow or scarce. With this work, I managed to solve the problem, achieving differences of less than 0.5 degrees between the two.
